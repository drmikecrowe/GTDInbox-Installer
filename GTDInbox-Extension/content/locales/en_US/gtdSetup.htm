1) Understand how GTDInbox uses labels

Status labels are used to action emails into tasks. They define the email's status (e.g. S/Next for high priority, S/Action to be done, S/Waiting On for delegated, S/Some Day for low priority, S/Finished for complete).

To get a good understanding of GTDInbox's simple work flow, and examples, read Getting Started.
