1) What are Status Labels?

GTDInbox uses Status labels (those that are prefixed with S/, such as S/Action, S/Waiting On) to turn emails into actions.

Any email that has a Status label (other than S/Finished) is active and requiring attention.

To get started, you must create the basic Status labels in Gmail.
